The Philippines is one of the most biodiverse countries on Earth,

but many species and habitats are at risk due to deforestation, pollution, and climate change.

Saribuhay aims to raise awareness and inspire Filipinos to protect our natural treasures.

Biodiversity gives us food, livelihood, and protection from disasters.

Protecting nature means protecting people,

and caring for the environment ensures a safer, greener future for all.
